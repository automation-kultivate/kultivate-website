This privacy policy explains how personal data is processed when you visit this website or contact us by email. Personal data means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.

3. Hosting via GitHub Pages

This website is intended to be hosted using GitHub Pages, a static website hosting service provided by GitHub, Inc., 88 Colin P. Kelly Jr. Street, San Francisco, CA 94107, United States.

When you access the website, technical data may be processed automatically by GitHub for the purpose of delivering the website securely and reliably. This may include your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring URL, requested files, date and time of access and similar server log data.

The legal basis is our legitimate interest in providing a secure, stable and efficient website, Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR.

Because GitHub is based in the United States, data may be transferred outside the European Economic Area. GitHub provides information on privacy and international data transfers in its privacy documentation.

More information: GitHub Privacy Statement

This website loads the Lenis smooth scrolling library from the jsDelivr CDN. When the library is loaded, technical information such as your IP address, browser information, requested file, date and time of access may be transmitted to the CDN provider.

We use this service to provide a performant and stable website experience. The legal basis is Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR.

More information: jsDelivr Privacy Policy
